About this project

The Extreme Metal Vocal Techniques Project was initially created as a supplementary tool for academic research on extreme vocal techniques in metal music, conducted as part of the Metal Music Research Project. However, over time, it evolved into an independent and constantly growing project, actively developed even today.

This project was born out of pure love for extreme heavy music and aims to provide structured, accessible, and interactive data for music researchers, musicians, and fans interested in the analysis of extreme vocal techniques.

All spreadsheets, audio files and everything related to this project is available on GitHub:
Extreme Vocal Techniques Database

Available Databases

  • Extreme Metal Vocal Techniques Database with Spotify popularity metrics: A curated database of metal vocal excerpts enriched with popularity metrics from Spotify.
  • Extreme Metal Vocal Techniques Extended Database: A large-scale database currently under development. GitHub repository will be published soon.
  • Extreme Metal Vocal Stimuli Database: A specialized database of studio-recorded vocal stimuli.
